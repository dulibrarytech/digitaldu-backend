A Sound Model

    • Settings 1
    • Settings 2

The "A Sound Model" project, begun by the Colorado Digitization Project in 2004 through a grant from the Institute of Museum and Library Services, contains thousands of digitized heritage items, focusing especially on audio resources and oral histories to provide a model for the digitization of audio resources by other collaborative or consortia programs. The University of Denver Libraries provide access to the content from the Sound Model project through this Islandora digital repository site.
